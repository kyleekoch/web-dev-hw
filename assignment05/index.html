The Best Chocolate Cake Ever

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • 3 cups of flour
  • 3 cups of sugar
  • 1.5 cups of unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 tablespoon baking soda
  • 1.5 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1.5 teaspoon salt
  • 4 eggs
  • 1.5 cups buttermilk
  • 1.5 cups warm water
  • 1.5 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degree and butter three 9 inch cake rounds. Lightly dust with flour
  2. Mix together flour, sugar, cocoa, baking soda, baking powder, and salt.
  3. Add eggs, buttermilk, warm water, oil, and vanilla. Beat on medium speed until smooth.
  4. Divide batter among the three pans.
  5. Bake for 30-35 minutes.
  6. Cool on racks for 15 minutes.
  7. Frost with your favorite frosting and enjoy!
